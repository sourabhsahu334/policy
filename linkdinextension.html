Privacy Policy for Neukaps LinkedIn Auto Connect

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

Neukaps ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Neukaps LinkedIn Auto Connect Chrome extension.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our extension is designed with a "Privacy First" approach.

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, password, or LinkedIn credentials.
  • Local Processing: Any data required for the extension to function (such as detecting "Connect" buttons on the page) is processed entirely within your local browser environment.
  • User Activity & Website Content: The extension interacts with your active LinkedIn tab to identify connection buttons and automate clicks. This information is used solely to perform the automation you request and is never saved or sent to any external servers.

2. Storage

The extension uses the chrome.storage.local API exclusively to save your session preferences (e.g., the number of connections you wish to send) and to track the progress of your current session. This data remains on your physical device and is not synchronized across different browsers or shared with us.

2. Data Transmission

Neukaps LinkedIn Auto Connect does not communicate with any external servers. There are no background analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party APIs involved that could transmit your data.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not—and cannot—sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties.

5. Security

While we do not collect data, we ensure that the extension operates securely within the permissions granted by the Chrome browser. We do not use remote code, ensuring all logic is transparent and local to your machine.

6. Compliance with Chrome Web Store Policies

This extension complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Single Purpose and Limited Use requirements.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
