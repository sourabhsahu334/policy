Privacy Policy — Fluenzy

Last updated: 24 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fluenzy ("we", "us", "our", or the "App"), published by Neukaps Softwares, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By downloading or using Fluenzy, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Account information — When you sign up or sign in (including via Google Sign-In), we collect your name, email address, and profile photo (if provided by your account).
  • Content you create — Speaking practice recordings, learning progress, lesson responses, and any preferences you configure in the App.

b. Voice & Microphone

  • Microphone / voice — Fluenzy uses your device microphone and speech-recognition features to let you practice speaking and receive feedback. Audio is processed to evaluate pronunciation and fluency.

c. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage & analytics data — App interactions, features used, session duration, and in-app events.
  • Device information — Device model, operating system version, language, unique device identifiers, and app version.
  • Diagnostics & crash data — Crash logs and performance data to help us fix problems and improve stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate you.
  • To provide, personalize, and improve the App's learning features.
  • To process speech input and deliver pronunciation and fluency feedback.
  • To send notifications (such as reminders and updates) where you have permitted them.
  • To analyze usage trends and improve app performance and reliability.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or abuse.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With the service providers listed above, solely to operate the App.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or valid government request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of our users, the public, or Fluenzy.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case we will notify you.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures — including encryption in transit and access controls — to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & update — You can view and update your profile information within the App.
  • Delete your account — You may request account and data deletion at any time by contacting us or using the in-app option where available.
  • Permissions — You can revoke microphone, camera, or notification permissions in your device settings at any time (some features may stop working).
  • Analytics — You may opt out of certain analytics collection where the App or your device provides that control.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Children's Privacy

Fluenzy is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country. By using the App, you consent to such transfers, which are carried out with appropriate safeguards.
