Delete Your Account & Data — Fluenzy

Last updated: 24 July 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Fluenzy account and associated data. Fluenzy is published by Neukaps Softwares. You can delete your account and personal data at any time using either of the methods below.

Option 1 — Delete in the App

  1. Open the Fluenzy app and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Profile (or Settings).
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the request. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted.

Option 2 — Request by Email

If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request from the email address linked to your account to:

Email a Deletion Request

Please include the email address associated with your account so we can verify your identity. We will process verified requests within 30 days.

What Data Is Deleted

When your deletion request is processed, we permanently delete the following:

Data Status
Account information (name, email, profile photo) Deleted
Speaking practice recordings and audio data Deleted
Learning progress, lesson responses, and preferences Deleted
Usage & analytics identifiers linked to your account Deleted or anonymized

What May Be Retained

We may retain a limited amount of information where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes (for example, transaction or abuse records). Such data is kept only for as long as legally required and is otherwise deleted or anonymized. Anonymized, aggregated data that can no longer identify you may be retained for analytics.
